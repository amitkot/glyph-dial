## Long description

Glyph Dial is a minimalist Hebrew analog watchface for Pebble Time 2 and Pebble Round 2.

Instead of standard numerals, the dial uses Hebrew markers in a clean modern layout, paired with long tapered hands and a calm high-contrast monochrome design.

The goal is simple: make a watchface that feels elegant, distinctive, and actually wearable every day. Glyph Dial keeps the screen focused on the essentials, with a strong typographic identity and a balanced analog composition designed specifically for the new Pebble hardware.

Features:
- Hebrew glyph dial
- Clean analog layout
- Optimized for Time 2 and Round 2
- High-contrast monochrome design
- Long tapered hands with a refined center hub

Glyph Dial is designed to feel less like a gadget skin and more like a real watch.
